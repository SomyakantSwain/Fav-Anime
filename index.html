MY Favourite Anime

Death Note

Death Note is a popular manga and anime series that revolves around a supernatural notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. The main character, Light Yagami, is a genius student who uses the notebook to eliminate criminals and create a new world order. However, he faces opposition from L, a mysterious detective who is determined to catch him

Naruto

Naruto Shippuden is the sequel to the original Naruto anime series. It follows the adventures of Naruto Uzumaki and his friends after he returns from his training with Jiraiya. Naruto Shippuden features new enemies, new allies, new powers, and new challenges for Naruto and his team.
